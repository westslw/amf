How it Works

  1. Customer submits its sensor specification to AMFitzgerald
  2. AMFitzgerald uses its verified reference designs to create a custom sensor
  3. Foundry manufactures sensors
  4. AMFitzgerald provides tested, known good chips to customer

We're pioneering a new model for designer-foundry collaboration which enables rapid and cost-effective development of MEMS sensors. Our innovation is a manufacturing-friendly approach to MEMS development: new sensors are designed specifically for existing foundry process capabilities.

AMFitzgerald has created reference pressure sensor designs optimized for a process flow assembled from Silex SmartBlocks™. Customer sensors will be fabricated in a multi-project wafer (MPW) run managed by AMFitzgerald. RocketMEMS® will enable customers to meet their system requirements, while significantly lowering risk and time to market with production-ready processes.
