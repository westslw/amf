RocketMEMS™

A New Era in MEMS Development

  • RocketMEMS Overview (PDF)
  • MEMS solutions for OEMs and system integrators
  • ISO certified foundries
  • Cost-effective multi project wafer runs
  • Rocket-fast time to market
  • Press Release: November 7, 2012

RocketMEMS™ offers a new development interface to customers who need MEMS chips. Customers submit a specification, and then RocketMEMS provides design and fabrication services to produce MEMS chips.

This is a new model for designer-foundry collaboration which enables rapid and cost-effective development of MEMS sensors. The program uses a manufacturing-friendly approach to MEMS development: new sensors are designed specifically for existing foundry process capabilities. AMFitzgerald is implementing the Silex SmartBlock® approach to process integration for the first offering from the RocketMEMS program, which will be semi-custom pressure sensors fabricated in a multi-project wafer (MPW) run. Customers will submit sensor specifications to AMFitzgerald, who has designed a sensor architecture to fit a process flow assembled from SmartBlocks. Sensor designs will then be tailored to fit individual customers' needs. RocketMEMS will enable customers to meet their system requirements, while significantly lowering risk and time to market with Silex's production-ready processes.
