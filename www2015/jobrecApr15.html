We're Hiring: Office Assistant

Job Description

Our successful and growing engineering services firm is looking for an office assistant. We have a quiet professional office with friendly people who value helpfulness, diligence, and integrity.

This job will require doing a variety of different things and attention to detail. This job could grow into a full time job, with accompanying benefits, as you prove yourself and as our company grows.

Job Requirements

  • Support our employees to help them do their work as efficiently as possible
  • Clerical work, such as scanning and printing documents
  • Shipping and receiving packages, including bringing packages to FedEx depot
  • Supporting occasional client meetings by ordering lunch, serving coffee and refreshments, making restaurant reservations
  • Running errands around town (bank, post office, supplies, etc.)
  • Office organizational projects: sorting and labeling documents, files, items
  • Familiarity with PC computers and Microsoft Office software
  • Must have reliable transportation
  • English fluency
  • U.S. Citizen or Permanent Resident (green card) only. Our company uses E-Verify to validate documents.

Desired Skills

  • Prior experience as a personal or office assistant
  • Skilled with Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint

Compensation

  • $20 per hour
  • Work Hours: 1:00pm - 5:00pm, Monday thru Thursday

Submitting an Application

  • Please email your resume to careers@amfitzgerald.com
  • The subject line of your email MUST contain the text: "Office Assistant"
  • Resume must be no more than one page
  • No phone calls, please!
  • Background check and references are required prior to hiring
