Your Partner in MEMS Development

Since 2003, AMFitzgerald has been a key development partner to over 125 clients around the world, ranging in size from startups to Fortune 100 companies. Our team of experts develops all types of MEMS sensors and actuators for applications in the consumer electronics, medical devices, industrial, aerospace, and scientific markets. MEMS devices designed and developed by AMFitzgerald are helping to save lives, improve quality of life, and reduce waste and inefficiency in industrial processes.

We also help shape our customers' business and product strategies by leveraging our deep technical knowledge and unique industry insight. We are well known in the MEMS industry for our commitment to excellence, our expertise and practicality, and for providing forthright assessment of risk. Our customers trust us with their most critical projects and return to us year after year.
